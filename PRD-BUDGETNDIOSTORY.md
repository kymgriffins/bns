## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

### 1.1 Product Overview

**Product Name:** Budget Ndio Story  
**Product Type:** Civic Education Web Platform  
**Platform:** Web Application (Responsive - Mobile, Tablet, Desktop)  
**Core Summary:** A narrative-driven storytelling platform that transforms Kenya's national and county budgets into clear, interactive financial stories and journey-based experiences, enabling youth participation and civic accountability.  
**Target Users:** Kenyan youth (18-35), civil society organizations, journalists, researchers, community activists, university students, and general public interested in budget transparency.

### 1.2 Problem Statement

Kenyan citizens, particularly youth, face significant barriers to understanding and engaging with public budgets:

- **Complex Documentation:** Budget documents are technical, lengthy, and written in financial jargon
- **Lack of Accessibility:** Key budget information is scattered across multiple sources and not available in accessible formats
- **Limited Participation:** Citizens lack clear pathways to participate in budget processes
- **No Tracking Mechanism:** No easy way to track whether allocated funds are actually delivered
- **Disconnect from Reality:** Budget numbers don't connect to real-world impacts on communities

### 1.3 Product Vision

Budget Ndio Story will become Kenya's leading platform for budget literacy and civic engagement by:

- Translating complex budget documents into clear, narrative-driven content
- Providing interactive tools to explore budget allocations and spending
- Creating journey-based experiences that guide users from understanding to action
- Building a community of informed, engaged citizens who can hold leaders accountable
