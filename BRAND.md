# Brand Identity & Global Network

## Global Brand Definition

### Brand Name
**Budget Ndio Story** - A Global Budget Transparency Initiative

### Tagline
"Follow the Budget. Find the Story. — Worldwide."

### Brand Vision
To become the world's leading platform for citizen-driven budget transparency, empowering communities globally to understand, track, and influence public finance for equitable development.

### Brand Mission
We transform complex government budgets into accessible, actionable insights through innovative technology, community engagement, and strategic partnerships—ensuring every citizen can participate in democratic financial oversight.

## Advertisements & Sponsorships

### Advertisement Guidelines

All advertisements and sponsored content must align with:
1. Brand values and mission
2. Transparency and integrity principles
3. No political advertising
4. No harmful product advertising
5. Clear disclosure of sponsored content

### Advertisement Categories
- **Campaign Sponsors** - Budget awareness campaigns
- **Event Partners** - Workshops, trainings, conferences
- **Media Partnerships** - Content distribution agreements
- **Corporate Sponsors** - CSR-aligned organizations
- **Institutional Funders** - Foundations, governments, multilaterals
